Resolve underspecified areas and ask clarifying questions.

This command helps identify gaps in requirements, specifications, or plans and systematically addresses them through targeted questions.

Usage:
/clarify [specification-id]

Options:
--all - Review all underspecified areas across the project
--latest - Focus on the most recent specification

Examples:
/clarify - Review current specification for gaps
/clarify --all - Check all specifications for completeness
/clarify 001-real-time-notifications - Focus on specific specification
