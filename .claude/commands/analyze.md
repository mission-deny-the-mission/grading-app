Validate consistency and coverage across specifications, plans, and tasks.

This command performs cross-artifact analysis to ensure completeness, consistency, and proper coverage of requirements.

Usage:
/analyze [options]

Options:
--coverage - Check requirement coverage
--consistency - Validate consistency between artifacts
--gaps - Identify gaps and missing elements
--all - Perform comprehensive analysis

Examples:
/analyze - Basic consistency check
/analyze --coverage - Verify all requirements are covered
/analyze --gaps - Identify missing components
/analyze --all - Full analysis suite
